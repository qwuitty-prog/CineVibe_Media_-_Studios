Questions

Frequently Asked

What is your typical project timeline?

+

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but generally: Essential packages take 2-3 weeks, Professional packages take 3-6 weeks, and Premium packages take 6-10 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the consultation phase and maintain regular communication throughout the process to ensure we stay on schedule.

Do you work with international clients?

+

Yes, we work with clients worldwide. We've successfully completed projects for companies in Europe, Asia, Australia, and across North America. We're experienced in managing time zone differences and can conduct meetings via video conference at times convenient for all parties. We also handle all file transfers through secure, high-speed servers.

What file formats do you deliver?

+

We deliver in all standard video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, and WebM. For broadcast, we can provide ProRes, DNxHD, and other industry-standard formats. We also deliver project files upon request (additional fees may apply for source file delivery). We ensure all deliverables are optimized for their intended use (web, social media, broadcast, etc.).

Can you work with our existing footage?

+

Absolutely. We frequently work with client-provided footage. During our initial consultation, we'll review your existing material and advise on how to best utilize it. If additional shooting is needed, we can arrange that as well. We can also enhance existing footage through color grading, stabilization, and visual effects to meet professional standards.

What is your revision policy?

+

Each package includes a specific number of revision rounds as outlined in the service agreement. Revisions are defined as changes requested after the initial delivery. We strive to get it right the first time through detailed briefs and checkpoints throughout the process. Additional revisions beyond the included amount are available at an hourly rate, which we disclose upfront.
