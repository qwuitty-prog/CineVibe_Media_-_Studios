Where Creativity Meets Technology

Founded in Los Angeles in 2012, CineVibe Media & Studios began with a simple mission: to create visually stunning content that captivates audiences and tells compelling stories. What started as a small editing studio has grown into a full-service production house specializing in video editing, 3D animation, and visual effects.

Our team of award-winning artists and technicians combines cutting-edge technology with artistic vision to deliver exceptional results for clients ranging from indie filmmakers to Fortune 500 companies. We believe that every project, no matter the scale, deserves the highest level of creative attention and technical precision.

Our Journey

Milestones & Achievements

The Beginning

CineVibe Media & Studios was founded in a small Los Angeles studio with a focus on independent film editing and motion graphics for local businesses.

2012

First Major Film Project

We provided visual effects for an indie sci-fi feature that gained critical acclaim at Sundance, establishing our reputation in the film community.

2015

Studio Expansion

Moved to our current 10,000 sq ft facility and expanded our team to include specialized 3D animation and advanced VFX departments.

2018

Industry Recognition

Won our first major industry awards for visual effects and animation, solidifying our position as a leader in premium video production.

2021

Global Reach
