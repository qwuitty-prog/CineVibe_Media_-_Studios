Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical response time for inquiries?

+

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent requests, you can call us directly at +1 (555) 123-4567. After submitting your project details through our contact form, you'll receive an automated confirmation email, followed by a personalized response from our team.

Do you offer free consultations?

+

Yes, we offer free 30-minute initial consultations for all new projects. During this consultation, we'll discuss your project goals, timeline, budget, and creative vision. This helps us understand your needs and provide an accurate quote. You can schedule a consultation through our contact form or by calling our studio directly.

What information should I include in my project inquiry?

+

For the most accurate quote, please include: Project description and goals, target audience, project length/duration, preferred style or visual references, budget range, timeline/deadline, and any existing assets or footage. The more detail you provide, the better we can understand your needs and provide an accurate estimate.

Do you work with international clients?

+

Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide. We have experience coordinating projects across different time zones and can accommodate various communication preferences (video calls, email, project management tools). We accept payments in multiple currencies and can deliver files through secure, high-speed transfer services regardless of location.

What is your typical project timeline?

+

Project timelines vary based on complexity, scope, and current workload. Simple editing projects can take 1-2 weeks, while complex animations or VFX-heavy projects can take 8-12 weeks or more. During our consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific project requirements and our current capacity.
